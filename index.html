ABOUT ME

Multimedia designer with a passion for aesthetics, UX, and functional design.

I work with Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Visual Studio Code, utilizing semantic HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and PHP to create intuitive, aesthetic, and user-friendly solutions. My approach is detail-oriented, with a focus on accessibility, functionality, and brand identity to ensure seamless digital experiences.

In addition, I have a deep interest in photography and photo editing, using CaptureOne and Adobe tools to enhance and refine visual storytelling. I also have experience working with WordPress, where I integrate design and development to build engaging digital platforms.

I am 24 years old, based in Copenhagen, and originally from Bornholm. Outside of work, I draw inspiration from cooking, coffee, art, ceramics, vinyl records, and nature, which influence my creative process and design approach.
